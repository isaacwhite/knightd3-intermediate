Traffic collisions in NYC, by month

July 2012 - September 2015

The NYPD keeps a record of vehicle collisions which are reported in NYC. Data includes time, precise location, borough, zip code, possible contributing factors to the incident, as well as categories of persons injured/killed. Individual data for each report has been reduced to aggregate totals of deaths, injuries, and reported incidents by month for this chart. Original data from NYC Open Data.
